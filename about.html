ABOUT ME

I'm Gabin Tavernier, a student from La Réunion passionate about aerospace, electronics, and building things that actually work.

I design custom PCBs with KiCad, program microcontrollers (RP2040, ESP32) in C/C++ and Python, and build everything from macropads to stratospheric balloon payloads with LoRa telemetry. I got my BIA (Aeronautics Initiation Diploma) in 2024 and I'm aiming for ISAE-SUPAERO.

I'm active in Hack Club — I've participated in programs like Blueprint, Stasis, and various YSWS grants to fund my hardware projects. Making things from scratch, understanding how they work at every level, and pushing them further is what drives me.
